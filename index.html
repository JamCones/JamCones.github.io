01 — About

Signal Theory
meets Practice

I'm Cameron — an audio software developer with a deep obsession for electronic instruments and the music made with them. My work sits at the crossroads of DSP theory, synthesis design, and the raw joy of plugging a cable into something and not knowing quite what will happen.

My current focus is VCV Rack — designing custom modules that explore unconventional approaches to signal processing, waveshaping, and generative sound design. Every module is an experiment in translating a mathematical idea into something you can hear.

Beyond the patch cables, I'm interested in the mathematics behind synthesis — wavetable algorithms, filter topology, spectral processing — and how these ideas can be shaped into expressive, playable instruments.
